The Kakis Programme

Not only do you get to enjoy discounts of up to 20% on alcoholic beverages across all our outlets, but your friends will too! Furthermore, 2% of your spendings will go into your bar credits, where every 1 point is equal to 1 dollar. And with these bar credits, you can use them to offset bills at any Georges outlet.

And that's not all! Be the first to know whenever we have our events and promotions. We have our monthly Kakis Night where you can expect great deals at the bar. On your birthday month, we will also send you vouchers that you can use at our outlets for your celebrations. We also send out vouchers via the app where you can receive and redeem them. You can also make reservations and leave a feedback, and connect with us on social media at your fingertips!

Lastly, we will be rolling out our MadBar Store soon where you can make purchases in the comfort of your home. Better yet, you get to use your bar credits to offset the bill! We will also be partnering with other merchants for discounts exclusive to Georges Kakis!

Whenever you're ready to come onboard, simply visit any Georges outlets and pay a one-time registration fee of $40 + GST and you will then receive an invitation code.

If you have any further questions, do feel free to look for any of our Branch Managers.
